About Our Library

Serving the Union Beach community with dedication and pride

Our Mission and Vision

Mission Statement

The Union Beach Memorial Library is dedicated to serving as a vibrant community hub that provides free and equal access to information, knowledge, and resources for all residents of Union Beach. We honor the memory of the twelve local heroes who gave their lives in World War II by fostering literacy, lifelong learning, and community connection. Through our collections, programs, and services, we strive to enrich lives, inspire curiosity, and support the educational, recreational, and cultural needs of our diverse community.

Vision Statement

We envision the Union Beach Memorial Library as the heart of our community—a welcoming space where residents of all ages come together to learn, grow, and connect. We aspire to be a leader in providing innovative library services and programs that adapt to the changing needs of our patrons while preserving the rich history and heritage of Union Beach. Through collaboration with local organizations and the Monmouth County Library System, we aim to create lasting positive impact and ensure that every community member has the resources they need to thrive.

Library Association Board of Trustees

Our dedicated Board of Trustees guides the mission and operations of the Union Beach Memorial Library. These community leaders volunteer their time and expertise to ensure our library continues to serve the residents of Union Beach with excellence.

Margaret Johnson

President, Board of Trustees

Margaret has served on the Library Association Board for over 15 years and has been President for the past 5 years. A retired educator with 35 years of teaching experience in Monmouth County schools, she is passionate about literacy and community education. Margaret has led numerous initiatives to expand children's programming and modernize library technology services. She holds a Master's degree in Education from Rutgers University and has been a Union Beach resident for 40 years.

Robert Martinez

Vice President, Board of Trustees

Robert joined the Board in 2018 and brings extensive financial expertise from his career as a Certified Public Accountant. He oversees the library's budget and financial planning, ensuring fiscal responsibility and sustainability. Robert is particularly committed to making library services accessible to all community members and has spearheaded fundraising efforts for library renovations. A Union Beach native, he returned to his hometown after working in New York City for 20 years and is dedicated to giving back to the community that shaped his life.

Sarah Chen

Secretary, Board of Trustees

Sarah has been serving on the Board since 2019 and handles all official records and communications. As a practicing attorney specializing in nonprofit law, she provides invaluable legal guidance to ensure the library operates in compliance with all state and federal regulations. Sarah is passionate about digital literacy and has helped establish computer training programs for seniors and new immigrants. She and her family moved to Union Beach in 2015, drawn by the tight-knit community and excellent local services. She holds a JD from Seton Hall Law School.

James O'Brien

Treasurer, Board of Trustees

James joined the Board in 2020 and serves as Treasurer, managing financial transactions and reporting. A small business owner who runs a local bookstore, James understands the importance of community gathering spaces and the role libraries play in fostering literacy and learning. He coordinates the library's book sales and donation programs, building strong relationships with local vendors and publishers. A Marine Corps veteran, James deeply appreciates the library's memorial to local heroes and works to honor their legacy through community service. He has lived in Union Beach for 25 years with his wife and three children.

Dr. Divya Patel

Trustee, Board Member

Dr. Patel joined the Board in 2021 and brings expertise in technology and innovation. As a computer science professor at a nearby university, she has been instrumental in developing the library's STEM programming for youth and introducing maker space initiatives. Dr. Patel is committed to bridging the digital divide and ensuring all community members have access to technology and digital resources. She regularly conducts coding workshops for children and teenagers at the library. Originally from New Jersey, she settled in Union Beach in 2017 with her husband and two sons, drawn to the excellent schools and strong community spirit. She holds a PhD in Computer Science from MIT.

Thomas Williams

Trustee, Board Member

Thomas has been a Board member since 2017 and is the youngest member of the current Board. A high school history teacher in the local school district, he is passionate about preserving Union Beach's history and making it accessible to younger generations. Thomas leads the library's local history digitization project and organizes community events celebrating Union Beach heritage. He has initiated partnerships with local schools to bring students to the library for research projects and historical education. A lifelong Union Beach resident, Thomas credits the library with inspiring his love of learning and is committed to ensuring future generations have the same opportunities. He holds a Master's degree in History and Education from Monmouth University.

Our Services and Programs

Collections

  • Books, audiobooks, and e-books for all ages
  • DVDs, CDs, and digital media
  • Magazines, newspapers, and periodicals
  • Local history archives and special collections

Technology Access

  • Public computers with internet access
  • Free WiFi throughout the library
  • Printing, scanning, and copying services
  • Technology assistance and training

Children's Programs

  • Story time for preschoolers
  • Summer reading program
  • Homework help and tutoring
  • STEM workshops and activities

Adult Programs

  • Book clubs and discussion groups
  • Computer and digital literacy classes
  • Author talks and literary events
  • Job search and career resources

Community Services

  • Meeting room rentals
  • Community bulletin board
  • Local resource referrals
  • Partnership with Monmouth County Library System

Special Collections

  • Union Beach historical archives
  • World War II memorial collection
  • New Jersey genealogy resources
  • Local newspaper archives

Visit Us Today

We welcome all Union Beach residents and visitors to explore our library, attend our programs, and take advantage of our services. Stop by during our operating hours or contact us to learn more.
